Intersection of Universes

At the crossroads of cybershamanism and worldmaking, we found pragmatic psychedelia. IoU is a conceptual framework for rethinking our relation to technology. This project draws freely on the imaginary relations between particle physics, animism, magic, digital technologies.

Intersection of Universes is supported by Art Council Tokyo.
